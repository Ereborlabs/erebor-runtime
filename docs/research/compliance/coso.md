# COSO

## What It Is For

COSO provides internal control and enterprise risk management frameworks. It is
useful for Erebor when agent actions affect control activities, monitoring,
financial processes, or GenAI risk governance.

## Evidence Companies Need

- control environment evidence
- risk assessment records
- control activities
- information and communication evidence
- monitoring activities
- issue and remediation tracking

## What Erebor Can Do

Erebor can provide control evidence for AI agents as part of internal control:
preventive gates, detective logs, approvals, segregation-of-duties enforcement,
exception monitoring, and remediation evidence, when actions are routed through
Erebor-controlled execution paths.

## Artifacts Erebor Could Generate

- AI-agent internal control evidence pack
- preventive/detective control operation report
- exception and override ledger
- segregation-of-duties action report
- remediation evidence bundle
- management monitoring dashboard

## Do Not Claim

Erebor does not implement COSO, evaluate enterprise risk management maturity,
own risk appetite, make management assertions, or guarantee
segregation-of-duties effectiveness outside governed workflows. It supports
specific control activities involving agent runtime behavior.
