# DCC

## What It Is For

DCC, UK Defence Cyber Certification, applies to UK defence suppliers under the
MOD Cyber Security Model and DEFCON 658 context. It is an organizational
assurance mechanism; Erebor can support evidence for governed workflows, not
replace the certification route.

## Evidence Companies Need

- risk assessment reference and cyber risk profile
- supplier assurance questionnaire evidence
- subcontractor flowdown evidence
- cyber improvement plans if required
- auditable evidence for required control levels
- certification evidence where applicable

## What Erebor Can Do

Erebor can provide auditable evidence for AI agents operating in
business-critical workflows, SaaS systems, browsers, terminals, cloud consoles,
or subcontractor-facing tooling, when those actions are routed through
Erebor-controlled execution paths.

## Artifacts Erebor Could Generate

- DEF STAN 05-138 agent-runtime evidence pack
- SAQ evidence appendix for AI-operated workflows
- cyber risk profile runtime-control coverage matrix
- cyber improvement plan tracker for agent-related gaps
- subcontractor flowdown evidence report
- business-critical operations dependency map for agents and tools

## Do Not Claim

Erebor is not a DCC certificate or certifying body. It cannot replace classified
data, OT, SAL, Cyber Essentials/Plus, SAQ/SCPS, RAR/CRP, flowdown, CIP, or
contract-specific requirements.
