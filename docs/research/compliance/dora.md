# DORA

## What It Is For

DORA is the EU regulation for digital operational resilience in the financial
sector. It applies from January 17, 2025 and covers ICT risk management,
incidents, resilience testing, ICT third-party risk, information sharing, and
oversight.

## Evidence Companies Need

- ICT risk framework evidence
- critical/important function mapping
- ICT asset and dependency records
- incident classification and reporting
- resilience testing evidence
- threat-led penetration testing where required
- third-party ICT register
- contractual controls
- recovery and continuity evidence

## What Erebor Can Do

Erebor can provide runtime governance for AI agents operating ICT systems,
financial SaaS tools, browsers, commands, and third-party platforms. It can
produce traceable evidence for controlled access, change execution, incident
timelines, resilience exercises, and tool usage tied to critical or important
functions, when those actions are routed through Erebor-controlled execution
paths.

## Artifacts Erebor Could Generate

- ICT agent activity register mapped to critical functions
- major ICT incident action timeline
- third-party SaaS/tool access report
- resilience test runbook plus execution transcript
- high-risk ICT change approval/rollback evidence
- outsourced AI/tool usage register

## Do Not Claim

Erebor does not replace a DORA ICT risk framework, legal incident reporting,
competent-authority submissions, TLPT providers, due diligence, contract
remediation, register-of-information ownership, full third-party risk
management, or resilience testing programs.
