# HIPAA

## What It Is For

HIPAA protects protected health information. The Security Rule focuses on
administrative, physical, and technical safeguards for electronic PHI.

## Evidence Companies Need

- risk analysis and risk management
- access authorization
- workforce controls
- audit controls
- information-system activity review
- security incident handling
- contingency planning
- business associate and subcontractor assurances
- breach documentation

## What Erebor Can Do

Erebor can govern AI agents that access healthcare systems, browsers, SaaS apps,
shells, APIs, or PHI-bearing workflows. The value is runtime evidence: what the
agent accessed, which policy applied, whether PHI export or mutation was
approved, and what was denied, when those actions are routed through
Erebor-controlled execution paths.

## Artifacts Erebor Could Generate

- AI-agent ePHI access audit log
- HIPAA Security Rule runtime-control evidence packet
- high-risk action approval and denial report
- governed-session evidence for incident investigation
- access-review exceptions and remediation register
- ePHI export/download trace report

## Do Not Claim

Erebor does not make an organization HIPAA compliant by itself. It does not
replace legal review, HIPAA risk analysis, PHI discovery/classification, BAAs,
training, sanctions, breach notification, or security controls outside the
governed runtime.
