# ISO/IEC 20000

## What It Is For

ISO/IEC 20000 is an IT service management standard. It is a valid support story
for AI-assisted service operations, but Erebor should not be positioned as an
ITSM platform or service management system.

## Evidence Companies Need

- service management system scope
- service delivery and support process evidence
- incident, request, change, and problem records
- service continuity and availability evidence
- supplier and configuration-management records
- audit and improvement evidence

## What Erebor Can Do

Erebor can capture agent actions in service operations: change execution,
incident remediation, service desk automation, SaaS administration, command
execution, approvals, and rejected actions, when those actions are routed
through Erebor-controlled execution paths.

## Artifacts Erebor Could Generate

- change evidence for AI-assisted service operations
- incident remediation log
- request automation audit
- denied service action register
- ticket-to-runtime reconciliation report
- service desk agent workflow evidence packet

## Do Not Claim

Erebor is not an ITSM platform, service desk, service management system, SLA
owner, configuration-management database, or ISO/IEC 20000 certification path.
It supplies runtime evidence for AI-assisted service workflows.
