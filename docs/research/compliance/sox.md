# SOX

## What It Is For

SOX is U.S. public-company financial reporting law. For Erebor, the relevant
area is management/auditor evidence for internal control over financial
reporting, including automated controls, IT-dependent manual controls, and
relevant IT general controls for systems in SOX scope.

## Evidence Companies Need

- CEO/CFO certification support
- ICFR design and operating-effectiveness evidence
- control testing
- access reviews
- change management
- segregation of duties
- journal-entry controls
- deficiency evaluation and remediation
- auditor-facing evidence packages subject to management and auditor evaluation

## What Erebor Can Do

Erebor can govern AI agents that touch ERP, finance SaaS, databases, scripts,
reporting pipelines, close workflows, privileged systems, APIs, MCP/tools, or
internal tools affecting financial reporting, when those actions are routed
through Erebor-controlled execution paths. It can preserve identity, approvals,
commands, browser/API/tool actions, data exports, blocked activity, and
exception rationale.

## Artifacts Erebor Could Generate

- financially relevant AI action log
- privileged-action and segregation-of-duties exception report
- change-control evidence pack for reporting systems
- agent/service-account access recertification report
- journal-entry, period-end reporting, and exception approval ledger
- control-testing population/sample export using approved criteria

## Do Not Claim

Erebor is not a SOX compliance solution, does not assess SOX scope, materiality,
significant accounts, deficiency severity, or ICFR effectiveness, and does not
replace management, COSO or another recognized control framework, legal counsel,
or auditors.
